Summer Internship 2019

Nestatini 2.0

Our interns were challenged to work on Nest’s internal management platform called Nestatini.

There, Nest Collective’s community members can visualize all the companies, co-workers and events that constitute our community and to manage our shareable resources. This way it would be easier to keep up with everything that is going on.
