Privacy Policy

Data Collected

We collect the following data, which includes personal data, from you:

— Personal identification information: Name, email address and phone number (collected on the application form);
— IP address (automatically collected);
— Web browser type and version (automatically collected);
— Operating system (automatically collected).

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide Nest Collective with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

— Voluntarily complete our application form to Nest Collective’s Summer Internship.
— Use or view our website via your browser’s cookies.

How will we use your data?

Nest Collective collects your data so that we can:

Process your application to our Summer Internship program and to manage the respective recruitment process accordingly.
Nest Collective may share your data with the partner companies that contribute to the organization of the Summer Internships so that they may include you in their own future recruitment processes.

How do we store your data?

It is our goal to guarantee the quality and integrity of the information provided by the users of our website. We have implemented the necessary measures, both at a technological and organizational level, in order to keep the information safe, accurate, updated and complete.

Nest Collective will keep your personal identification information for five years. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data by deleting all the answers provided in the respective application form.

What are your data protection rights?

Nest Collective would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access: You have the right to request Nest Collective for copies of your personal data.

The right to rectification: You have the right to request that Nest Collective correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Nest Collective to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure: You have the right to request that Nest Collective erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that Nest Collective restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to Nest Collective processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability: You have the right to request that Nest Collective transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
