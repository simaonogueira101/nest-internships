Summer Internship 2016

AURORA

An Android app to make check-ins in concerts to help you build an overview of the gigs you’ve collected in the past. In other words, a shareable passport of the concerts you’ve been to. People would also be able to follow artists, receive suggestions of nearby concerts and schedule events they wish to attend.

01 /
Let's learn new stuff

Design Sprint

Started on a Monday morning and with a giant sleep deprivation since Portugal had just been crowned the champion of Europe. The Design Sprint week aimed to shape the project ideas using Google Venture’s Design Sprint and some IDEO’s Design Thinking based processes.

02 /
Work, work, work

Development

The focus was on sharing knowledge with our interns about software development processes, along with new technologies and tools. For this Android app, they have developed their backend on Ruby on Rails and had to design an API.
